Features

  • Multi-user support & backups

    Export your PRSS site's database to a secure GitHub private repository and effortlessly import it to another computer or share with team members.

  • Easy to use Rich Text Editor (WYSIWYG)

    We're committed to providing a distraction-free writing experience. Our editor is intentionally simple and intuitive, mirroring the interfaces you're familiar with. And when you need to dive into your creative workflow, our Full Screen view lets you do just that – without any interruptions or digital noise.

  • AI-Powered Content Assistance

    The AI Integration addon empowers you to harness artificial intelligence for a range of creative tasks, including rewriting text segments, expanding sentences, and generating entire articles.

    Use this innovative tool to amplify your creativity, streamline your workflow, and produce high-quality content at an unprecedented scale – all tailored to your unique preferences.

  • Customize Your Site's Look and Feel

    Elevate your site's dynamic content and menus with ease, adding custom HTML, scripts, and styles as needed.

    Take your site to the next level by swapping out Themes or creating custom ones that perfectly align with your unique vision and requirements.

  • Fast and Responsive

    PRSS operates locally on your computer, eliminating the need for server latency common with platforms like WordPress or Ghost.

    This means that creating and editing your sites will feel incredibly snappy, allowing you to work efficiently without interruptions.

  • World-wide availability and speed

    With GitHub Pages support, your blog is automatically integrated into a worldwide Content Distribution Network (CDN), ensuring fast load times for readers everywhere.

    No need for caching plugins or performance optimizations like those found on WordPress – PRSS sites are optimized for speed right out of the box.

  • You own your data, no Registration Required

    Unlike many blogging platforms, PRSS doesn't require registration – you can start creating right away.

    Moreover, all your site's data is safely stored on your local machine, eliminating the risk of hackers gaining access to your blog's Admin Panel, a common concern with other platforms.

  • Optimized for search engines

    PRSS generates automatic sitemaps and meta tags for each site, ensuring seamless accessibility to search engines.

    You also have the flexibility to customize your own meta tags, allowing you to fine-tune your SEO strategy to meet specific requirements.

  • Free and Ad-Free

    PRSS is offered completely at no cost – we don't sell ads or your data, and never will.

    You can choose to purchase optional addons within the app, giving you more control over your experience.
