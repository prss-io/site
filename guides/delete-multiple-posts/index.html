How to delete multiple posts

Go to your site Dashboard and click on "Posts".

Then, click on the "Toggle Select" button on the top-right action bar, select your posts, and click the trash can icon.

That's all. Your blog is tidier now!

  • Note: You can also delete an individual post from the Post Editor sidebar.
