AI Companion Context Menu is Back

Published on 26 Aug, 2025
Photo by Alex Andrews via Pexels

Users subscribed to the free AI Companion addon will be glad to see the context menu back in their Default editor. The functionality was disabled earlier pending an upgrade of the editors. We are excited to bring the feature back for your enjoyment.


Subscribe to the AI Companion (formerly PRSSAI)

The AI Companion is one of these truly no-brainer addons. It is free to install and use, and uses your local AI installation to generate outputs. This means you don't have to pay any AI provider to use it. Moreover, PRSS Site Creator comes with a built-in Wizard to help you setup a local AI installation (Ollama) in a breeze.

To run AI locally, a good graphics card is necessary for optimal performance; otherwise, Ollama may be slow. For detailed requirements, refer to Ollama's documentation. If your computer specs are insufficient, consider acquiring an OpenAI API key and entering it in the AI Companion setup.
