PRSS Autoblogger Addon Updates

Published on 24 Aug, 2025
Photo by Pavel Danilyuk via Pexels

Your trusty Autoblogger has been updated

The PRSS Autoblogger addon has undergone some reliability updates, to ensure posts are created at the frequency that the user scheduled. There are also improvements to the UX to better signal and handle when a run is overdue. That is not all: new features are in the works for Autoblogger, such as hourly scheduling of posts (currently the most frequent option is "Daily").

To stay ahead of the curve, be sure to keep an eye on this blog for updates and announcements about new features and enhancements. And if you haven't already, subscribe to the addon from within your PRSS App to start experiencing its many benefits!

With PRSS Autoblogger addon, you'll be able to:

  • Create posts with a topic of your choice.
  • Choose between many posting frequencies, with "Hourly" to be added soon.
  • Choose if you want to auto publish or if the posts should be created for your later review.
  • Automatically add a fitting featured image for your post, sourced from Pexels.
  • Add attributions to your sources automatically or manually.
  • And more!
